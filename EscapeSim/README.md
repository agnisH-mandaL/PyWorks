## Escape Simulator

#### A graphical simulation of a "robot" traversing to reach the exit point of the obstacle layout.

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/agnisH-mandaL/PyWorks/main/EscapeSim/EscSim1.png" alt="Demo" width="600" height="450">


### *Description:* ###
- The "robot" enters from the (0,0) point towards positive x-axis only
- Upon stumbling upon an obstacle or boundary, it takes a right turn only
- If the "robot" reaches a point where both right and front are blocked either by obstacles or boundary or both, it is "stuck".
- At the 2nd row from the last on columns 1 and n, lies the exit points (chosen arbitrarily)
- The "robot" has a "battery" life of 25, which decreases by one with each step, i.e. simply it can only move upto 25 steps
- The size of the grid is determined by the user
